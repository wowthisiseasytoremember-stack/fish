### Findings

- **Compatibility Audit:** Fast-growing shingling or climbing plant. Ideal for background coverage in humid terrariums.
- **Liquidation Pricing:** 
    - **R. hayi:** $20–$40.
- **Care Notes:** Provide a flat, moisture-retentive support such as a cedar plank or cork slab. This species is a slow to moderate grower that thrives in bright indirect light and requires regular misting to maintain its tight shingling appearance.
