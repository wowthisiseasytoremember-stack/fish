# Tank/Inhabitant Task: *Monstera karstenianum* 'Peru'

**Location:** Center (Freestanding) — Rack 4 -> Shelf A (top)

## Inventory Entry
- *Monstera karstenianum* 'Peru'

## Goal
1. **Compatibility Audit:** Analyze species compatibility and downsizing/consolidation recommendations.
2. **Liquidation Pricing:** Estimate current market value for the animals and the equipment (tank size/type).
3. **Care Notes:** Provide a professional care summary for a potential buyer.

---

### Findings

- **Compatibility Audit:** Robust tropical climber. Excellent for high-humidity environments; provide support for growth. Toxic if ingested.
- **Liquidation Pricing:** 
    - **"Peru":** $15–$30.
- **Care Notes:** This species is sensitive to overwatering; ensure the substrate is very well-draining and allow it to dry out significantly between waterings. Provide a moss pole to support its climbing habit and to encourage the development of larger, more textured foliage.
