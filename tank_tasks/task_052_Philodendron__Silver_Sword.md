## Goal

### Findings

- **Compatibility Audit:** Generally safe for tropical vivariums or as houseplants. Provides excellent cover; toxic if ingested by pets or children.
- **Liquidation Pricing:** 
    - **P. 'Silver Sword':** $25–$45 (depending on size/maturity).
- **Care Notes:** A vigorous climber that requires a moss pole or trellis to develop its mature, elongated leaf shape. Provide bright indirect light and a chunky, well-aerated potting mix to ensure healthy root development.
