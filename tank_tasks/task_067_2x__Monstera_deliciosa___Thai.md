### Findings

- **Compatibility Audit:** Robust tropical climber. Excellent for high-humidity environments; provide support for growth. Toxic if ingested.
- **Liquidation Pricing:** 
    - **"Thai Constellation" (2):** $200–$400 ($100–$200 each depending on size).
- **Care Notes:** These plants are prone to root rot; utilize a highly porous, chunky substrate and allow the pot to dry out completely between waterings. The variegation is genetically stable, ensuring the plant retains its signature starry pattern as it matures.
