# Tank/Inhabitant Task: *Scindapsus pictus*

**Location:** Center (Freestanding) — Rack 4 -> Shelf A (top)

## Inventory Entry
- *Scindapsus pictus*

## Goal
1. **Compatibility Audit:** Analyze species compatibility and downsizing/consolidation recommendations.
2. **Liquidation Pricing:** Estimate current market value for the animals and the equipment (tank size/type).
3. **Care Notes:** Provide a professional care summary for a potential buyer.

---

### Findings

- **Compatibility Audit:** Trailing tropical plant with silver variegation. Thrives in moderate to high humidity; safe for most reptile/amphibian setups.
- **Liquidation Pricing:** 
    - **S. pictus:** $10–$20.
- **Care Notes:** Prefers bright indirect light and well-draining soil. Allow the top inch of soil to dry out between waterings; its silver-mottled leaves are sensitive to overwatering and will curl if the plant is thirsty.
