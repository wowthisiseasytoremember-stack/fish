### Findings

- **Compatibility Audit:** Aggressive, self-cloning crayfish. Will rapidly overpopulate and eat any tank mates or plants they can catch. Best in species-only tanks.
- **Liquidation Pricing:** 
    - **15 Gallon Setup & Colony:** $20–$30.
- **Care Notes:** Provide a complex environment with numerous hides like terracotta pots to protect molting individuals from cannibalism. A heavy, secure lid is essential as these crayfish are notorious for climbing out of tanks.
