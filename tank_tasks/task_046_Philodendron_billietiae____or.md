- *Philodendron billietiae* — orange petiole, smooth form

## Goal
1. **Compatibility Audit:** Analyze species compatibility and downsizing/consolidation recommendations.
2. **Liquidation Pricing:** Estimate current market value for the animals and the equipment (tank size/type).
3. **Care Notes:** Provide a professional care summary for a potential buyer.

---

### Findings

- **Compatibility Audit:** Generally safe for tropical vivariums or as houseplants. Provides excellent cover; toxic if ingested by pets or children.
- **Liquidation Pricing:** 
    - **P. billietiae:** $80–$150 (depending on size and petiole color).
- **Care Notes:** Thrives in bright indirect light and requires a chunky, well-draining aroid mix. The signature orange petioles are most vibrant when the plant is provided with a moss pole for support and consistent nutrient levels.
