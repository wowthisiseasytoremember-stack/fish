## Findings
- **Compatibility Audit:** Highly aggressive and territorial, especially during spawning. Requires a large tank or species-only setup to manage aggression.
- **Liquidation Pricing:**

  - **Equipment:** $10–$15.
- **Care Notes:** Provide plenty of rockwork and caves for territory. They are hardy but will dig, so secure any plants or heavy decor to the glass.
