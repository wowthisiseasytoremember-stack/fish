### Findings

- **Compatibility Audit:** Versatile tropical plant. Suitable for terrariums with high humidity; can be pruned to maintain size. Toxic if ingested.
- **Liquidation Pricing:** 
    - **S. 'Santa Maria':** $15–$25.
- **Care Notes:** Maintain in bright indirect light to preserve the leaf patterning. Keep the soil consistently moist but never waterlogged; these plants are rewarding growers and respond well to regular pruning to maintain a bushy shape.
