FISH ROOM DOWNSIZING SALE — CONTENT BRIEF
==========================================

PURPOSE
-------
Owner of a large home fish room is downsizing their entire collection to cover unexpected vet bills for their cat. Everything must go. Goal is finding responsible, knowledgeable homes for the animals — not just moving inventory fast. Owner has years of experience and has cared for all animals personally.

SITE GOAL
---------
A public-facing adoption/sale listing site. Visitors browse what's available, learn basic care info, and contact the owner to inquire. Animals and tanks/equipment are sold separately — someone can buy just a fish, just a tank, or both together.

PRICING NOTE
------------
All prices negotiable. The more someone takes, the bigger the discount. Bundle deals encouraged.

CONTACT
-------
Owner accepts inquiries via Reddit username, email, and a contact form. No location info is displayed publicly.

EXTRA INVENTORY NOTE
--------------------
The site lists the main animals and equipment, but the owner also has a large amount of unlisted items: substrates, spare terrariums, fertilizers, medications, CO2 equipment, hardscape (rocks, wood, cork bark), and more. Interested buyers should ask.
