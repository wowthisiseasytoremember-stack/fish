# Tank/Inhabitant Task: *Epipremnum aureum* "Lime Green"

**Location:** Center (Freestanding) — Rack 4 -> Shelf A (top)

## Inventory Entry
- *Epipremnum aureum* "Lime Green"

## Goal
1. **Compatibility Audit:** Analyze species compatibility and downsizing/consolidation recommendations.
2. **Liquidation Pricing:** Estimate current market value for the animals and the equipment (tank size/type).
3. **Care Notes:** Provide a professional care summary for a potential buyer.

---

### Findings

- **Compatibility Audit:** Hardy and fast-growing vine. Great for nutrient export in aquariums (roots in water) or as terrarium cover. Toxic if ingested.
- **Liquidation Pricing:** 
    - **"Lime Green" Pothos:** $10–$20.
- **Care Notes:** The vibrant neon color is best maintained in bright indirect light; in lower light conditions, the foliage may revert to a more standard green. Like all Pothos, it is very drought-tolerant and easy to propagate.
