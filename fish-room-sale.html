Why We're Selling

Downsizing Sale

ABIGAIL PHOTO
coming soon

Our cat Abigail Breslin passed away recently at 13 years old. She was beautiful and strong and so smart and opinionated, and we did everything we could for her. We have about $4,000 in vet bills left from her care that still need to be paid.

So we're downsizing the entire fish room — everything. These animals and setups have been years in the making, and this isn't a fire sale. We want them going to people who will actually care for them. That's the only thing that matters here.

On pricing: we're not trying to make money. If you're in a position to pay full price or put something toward Abigail's vet fund, that means a lot and we'd be genuinely grateful. But if you're stretched thin right now — like most people are — we're happy to work something out. The animals having good homes is worth more than the dollar amount.

Message us. We'll figure it out together.
